## The Problem
Industrial robotics is bleeding money on expensive depth-cameras and lengthy calibration. We are solving this bottleneck. Our software unlocks flawless, depth-level grip stability using standard, highly efficient RGB cameras—conquering the chaos of cluttered factory bins for a fraction of the cost.

## The Innovation
Applying breakthrough multi-view architecture published in January 2026, our custom fusion layer triangulates features from synchronized RGB views to instantly compute highly precise 3D objects without the 3D hardware price tag. 

## In This Demo
Watch our hardware officially mounted onto Ford's UR10e. The arm executes an orbital scan, capturing different angles of the target item. Our application instantly processes these captures to synthesize a 3D voxel cloud directly from standard 2D photos, mapping the object's spatial geometry. The intelligence then loops back, allowing the arm to execute a perfectly calculated, data-driven grasp. 

## Evaluation Highlights
* **Impact:** Reduces sensing complexity; standard RGB cameras can handle cluttered bins.
* **Innovation:** Computes 3D poses without 3D hardware using a January 2026 multi-view architecture.
* **Performance:** Maintains real-time processing and achieves high grasp stability during subsystem validation.
* **Technical Execution:** A decoupled pipeline where the vision engine runs independently and streams exact coordinates to the hardware.
* **Feasibility:** Built with off-the-shelf webcams, offering a highly cost-effective path for future industrial deployment.
